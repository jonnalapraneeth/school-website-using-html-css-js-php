ABOUT US

Excellent Educational Institutions,founded by late Afzal Basha, is one of the biggest names in the educational services sector in Bhadadri Kothagudem district -Telangana.The academy currently has 6 schools spreaded all over bhadri kothagudem district with a combined student population of approximately 5,000+.

Excellent Educational Institutions was founded in the year 1997 with the untiring attempts of our staff, constant support of parents and zealous performance of our students this small beginning became big and touched the pinnacle of success.with 24 years of educational eminence in teaching and with experience teaching staff we helped our students to achive there goals and reach success .in our institutions we focus on student all round devlopment .with 23 years of successfull run we helped our students to reach heights in Sports,Quiz programs,State level compitative exams and many more
